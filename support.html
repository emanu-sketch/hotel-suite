Frequently Asked Questions

What are check-in and check-out times?
Check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out is until 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out are available upon request.
Do you offer airport transfers?
Yes, we provide complimentary airport transfers for suite bookings. For other rooms, it's available at $50 per way.
Is there a cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 48 hours before arrival. After that, first night's rate applies.
Do you have vegetarian/vegan options?
Absolutely! Our restaurants offer extensive vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free menus.
Is the pool heated?
Yes, our infinity pool is heated year-round and open from 6 AM to 10 PM.
